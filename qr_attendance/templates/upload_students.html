Add Multiple Students (Batch Entry)

Enter one student per line in this format: roll_number, name, year, branch, dob (DDMMYYYY), section(optional)

📝 Example:

23CS1001, John Doe, 3, CSE, 15022005, A
23EC2002, Jane Smith, 2, ECE, 20072006, B
23ME3003, Mike Johnson, 4, MECH, 30112004
23CE4004, Sarah Williams, 1, CIVIL, 10032007, C

Note: DOB must be in DDMMYYYY format (8 digits, no separators)

📌 Batch Entry Guidelines:

  • Each line should have 5 or 6 comma-separated values
  • Order: roll_number, name, year, branch, dob, section (optional)
  • DOB must be 8 digits in DDMMYYYY format (e.g., 15022005 for 15th Feb 2005)
  • No hyphens, slashes, or spaces in DOB
  • Lines starting with # are ignored (comments)
  • Empty lines are ignored
